INNOVATION CENTRE

- The Innovation Centre has been set-up to promote innovative work in the Institute to create better talented, creative, and well-rounded engineers.The Centre will offer opportunities to augment your theoretical knowledge through a practice-based curriculum thereby making you more competitive for the job market. It will also give an opportunity to connect your innovative work with real world problems / industry needs and will also lead to the creation of market ready projects.

About Us

- Welcome to the heart of innovation and design! The UI/UX Club at KIET Group of Institutions is a dynamic community of creative minds passionate about crafting exceptional user experiences and stunning interfaces. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and human interaction by fostering a culture of design thinking and continuous learning.

What We Do

Design Workshops and Sessions:

Dive deep into the world of design with hands-on workshops covering the latest tools and trends.
Learn from industry experts and professionals who share their insights and experiences.

Project Collaborations

Collaborate on real-world projects, applying design principles to solve challenges and create impactful solutions.
Build a diverse portfolio that showcases your skills and creativity

Hackathons and Design Challenges

Participate in hackathons and design challenges to put your skills to the test and win exciting prizes.
Challenge yourself and your peers to think outside the box and push the boundaries of creativity.

Networking Events:

Connect with like-minded individuals, industry professionals, and alumni through networking events.
Gain valuable insights and build a strong professional network within the design community.
